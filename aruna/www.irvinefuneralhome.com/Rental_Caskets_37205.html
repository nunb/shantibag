Families are increasingly requesting to rent a casket. To facilitate this request we offer ceremonial caskets designed to allow for this service. These are specially crafted to facilitate an inner simple container to hold the deceased (metal offering excluded). The casket itself acts as a "shell" around this container making it suitable for public visitation and viewing.


 

After the funeral service, the funeral director removes the inner container from the outer casket shell. A separate lid is placed on the inner container holding the deceased and this is transported to the crematorium.

The outer casket is reused for a number of services. Eventually the casket reaches a point of wear and it is destroyed. Because its usage is limited, the ceremonial casket is replaced after a relatively small number of uses.
