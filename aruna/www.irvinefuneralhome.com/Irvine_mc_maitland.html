Irvine Memorial Chapel and Reception Centre
What began as a cremation centre complete with a crematorium, columbarium and gathering space in 2002 has been transformed into a complete facility with a new name reflecting local family ownership. Accommodating visitation, chapel ceremonies and receptions in spacious surroundings, our facility saves guests finding their way between various addresses.
Visitation Rooms
Our facility can accommodate a traditional visitation either with the casket or urn present.  All visitation areas incorporate the latest technology for including personalized video tributes of photos, or music of your choice.
Chapel
Our spacious chapel provides seating for 150 guests and additional seating is available for many more.  The chapel includes a premium audio system for music of your choice, a keyboard for piano or organ, and a flat panel monitor allowing a video tribute to be part of the ceremony.  Ceremonies may be digitally recorded to DVD upon request.
Reception Centre
Our reception centre can accommodate everything from an informal gathering to a sit-down meal.  Complete kitchen and catering services are available along with an outdoor patio where receptions may be held on a seasonal basis.
Crematorium
Ours is the only facility of its kind in our region, offering cremation while incorporating the crematorium within our building.  This minimizes delays and provides tremendous peace of mind for a family, knowing their loved one never leaves our care when cremation is requested.
